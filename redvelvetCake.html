Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor suger
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely grandulated white sugar.
Self-raising flour
A pre-mix combination of flour and leavening agents(usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 ℃
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium blow, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 mintues in thhe preheat oven, or untill top of the cake springs back when lightly pressed
  7. Cool in the pan oven a wire rack.
